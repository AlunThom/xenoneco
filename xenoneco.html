ECO4

ECO4

The ECO4 Scheme aims to provide free energy-saving measures for vulnerable households to improve the efficiency and heating of their home.

Through ECO4, eligible homes receive grants for home insulation, upgraded boiler or green heating system (solar, air source heat pumps).

ECO4 is funded through energy companies who are obliged by statute to contribute toward improving fuel poverty.
