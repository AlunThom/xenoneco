eco4

ECO4 is the fourth phase of the Government’s Energy Company Obligation. It started in August 2022 and it's due to continue until 31 March 2026.

The ECO4 Scheme aims to provide free energy-saving measures for vulnerable households to improve the efficiency and heating of their home. The Energy Company Obligation (ECO4) is a government energy-efficiency scheme. Through ECO4, eligible homes receive grants for home insulation, upgraded boiler or green heating system (solar, air source heat pumps).
ECO4 is funded through energy companies who are obliged by statute to contribute toward improving fuel poverty. This includes British Gas, EDF Energy, E.ON, NPower, Scottish Power and SSE.
ECO4 focusses on improving the least energy efficient homes and households in fuel poverty, as only properties in bands D-G can be treated. It also aims to deliver a more complete upgrade of those homes, shifting to a multi-measure whole-house retrofit approach.

Who's eligible for ECO4?

We will help complete assessments before installation and fully assess if you meet the relevant requirements. Whilst there are nuances the key criterias are:
  • An EPC rating of D or below
  • Receive at least one of the following benefits:
  • Child Benefits
  • Pension Guarantee Credit
  • Income-related Employment
  • Support Allowance (ESA)
